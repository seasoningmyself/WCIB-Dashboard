    How dated rates work: Each Pay Sheet looks up the rate in effect on its close date. So if you change Kaylee’s rate effective June 1, her May Pay Sheet (closed in May) uses the old rate, and her June Pay Sheet uses the new one. Past rate history is never deleted — it’s the audit trail.

    Settings

    Admin only · Office locations & data backup

    Office locations

    The office locations available on the Check Turn-In form. With more than one active location, the turn-in shows an “Office location” field so each check-in is tagged to the right office. You currently have one office, so that field stays hidden. Archiving hides a location from new turn-ins while keeping it on past records.

    Back up & restore — take your data between computers

    Your months live in this browser on this computer — the file alone won’t carry them. To work from another computer, click Back up to save a WCIB-data…json file (keep it in OneDrive), then Restore it on the other machine. Tip: back up when you finish on a computer so you never edit an older copy. The reminder in the corner tracks this for you.
